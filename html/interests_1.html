SPEEDRUNNING

A speedrun is a play-through of a whole video game or a selected part of it (such as a single level), performed with the intention of completing it as fast as possible.

I have a huge interest in video games in general, but one thing I’ve been getting into is speedrunning which is fairly niche. When I played Resident Evil 2, a survival horror game that I loved, I decided to get into the speedrunning community of the game (my first speedrun game). I did this by watching streamers on the platform Twitch who also speedran the game, learning from them and meeting many great people along the way.


Speedrun.com

Speedrun.comis a website where speedrunners share their times and compete on a leaderboard for specific categories in certain games.
